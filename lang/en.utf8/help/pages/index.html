Skillshare

List your skills for collaborations, or advertise for skills required. You can show up to four image examples of your work.

Once you've filled out the fields in the form you can drag a skillshare block onto your profile page when editing it. Also, if you check the 'Publish to Skillshare Directory' box, your skills listing will be viewable in the Skillshare Directory, which can be seen by logged-out users as well as workflow users.
